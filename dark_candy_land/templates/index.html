Rules

  • Setup: Shuffle, place pawns at Start, 32 teeth each, 0 candy.
  • Turn order: Click the draw card button.
  • Draw and move: Match card color .
  • Special spaces: some spaces have consquences
  • Special cards: With eating candy the cards are against you.
  • Candy vs. teeth: Brush Square revive 20 teeth.
  • Winning: Land exactly on the Castle with at least 1 tooth the dentist will fix your teeth.
